
Revolutionizing Real Estate with Blockchain

In the world of finance, Satoshi and Billy revolutionized money, while Vitalik and frens created a deentralized internet. The next big challenge lies in Real Estate, the world's largest asset class. 

Outdated systems have long plagued the industry, causing unnecessary hardships for people, particularly in less free societies where citizens can't trust middlemen to enforce their private property rights against big players, particularly power hungry governments: the gatekeepers to your private property ownership. 

The goal is to replace legacy systems through incentivization (cheaper/faster/better) to place our real estate ownership records on-chain. In Arizona, where I broker real estate, and a handful of forward-thinking states, smart contracts are legally enforceable. I'll be testing rollout in Arizona.

By leveraging smart contracts and using NFT deeds, digital identity verifiers, decentralized file storage, e signatures, AI for contract (ERC20 metadata) legal updates. All-in-one, P2P real estate platform. Buyers are able to send offers to Sellers within the dApp. Buyers pay the "write" eth gas, and sellers read, so no gas. After acceptance, a "pay earnest deposit to escrow" function, both of which encourage serious buyers only.

My dApp will be FREE FOR USERS, OPEN SOURCE, and DECENTRALIZED. It will work from a lot-line color-coded (NFT deed or not) map. It will offer legal contracts specific to each region and uses a hash identifier so on chain data is minimized to only eSigned contract pages & minted NFT upon recordation.

Local third-party intermediaries such as realtors, lenders, inspectors, appraisers, and title companies can pay a fee for the lead generation from the platform, if the user requests their assistance. My DApp auto generates area vendors by: A. promoting registered users (vendors) first and B. pulling them off of the internet using AI, and building an in-app reputation rating algorithm so that the vendors people like are pushed to the top. This includes brokers, realtors, lenders, inspectors, appraisers, title companies, pool companies, ac companies, roof companies, cleaning companies, landscaping companies, and can even allow front end room for advertising hardware and furniture stores, etc. The ads will pay for the app, not the users.

The platform will be built on Dogechain, based on Polygon Edge and using Solidity, with transactions conducted using Dogecoin for low fees and fast transactions. Also because I love Elon Musk so I'm using Dogecoin. This dApp will revolutionize the real estate industry and contribute to a more decentralized and equitable global economy, allowing people who cannot afford or do not want to use third-party intermediary services to transact freely.
